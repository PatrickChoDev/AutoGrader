## [1.0.0] - 2021-12-10
### Added
- `Run` and `Test` config commands.
- Support both compile and script languages.
- Full automate runner for [Python](https://www.python.org), [Rust](https://www.rust-lang.org), [GCC and G++](https://gcc.gnu.org)
